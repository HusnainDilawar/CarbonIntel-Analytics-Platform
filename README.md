Project Overview
CarbonIntel Analytics Platform is an enterprise sustainability intelligence and carbon accounting solution developed by Stellar Solutions to help organizations monitor, analyze, and optimize their carbon emissions. The platform transforms complex emissions datasets into actionable environmental insights through automated carbon calculations, interactive dashboards, and Net-Zero progress evaluation.
Designed for corporate sustainability teams, environmental consultants, ESG analysts, compliance officers, and decision-makers, the platform provides a transparent and auditable approach to carbon accounting while supporting strategic climate action planning.
